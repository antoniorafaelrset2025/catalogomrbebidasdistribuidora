# **App Name**: Online Showcase

## Core Features:

- Product Listing: Display the list of products with relevant details such as name, description, and price.
- Product Details: Provide a detailed view of each product, including multiple images and customer reviews.
- Search and Filtering: Enable users to easily find products using search functionality and filtering options by category, price range, etc.
- AI Product Recommendations: Suggest relevant products to users based on their browsing history and purchase behavior.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft Blue (#A0D2EB) for a calming and trustworthy feel, evoking the feeling of reliability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0), to give prominence to the elements in the foreground.
- Accent color: Muted Purple (#B39DDB), to highlight important interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, to impart a machined, objective, neutral look.
- Use clear, simple icons to represent product categories and filter options.
- Clean and organized layout to showcase products effectively. Prioritize product images and key information.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience, such as image carousel animations and loading indicators.
